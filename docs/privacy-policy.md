# Privacy Policy — StatFocus

_Last updated: 2026-05-21_

StatFocus is built around a simple principle: **your data stays on your device**.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** StatFocus does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data, usage data, or analytics with us or any third party.

All of the following stays exclusively on your Mac:

- Pomodoro session history (when you focused, for how long)
- Focus / break duration preferences
- Daily and weekly goals
- Language preference
- Backup folder location (if you configured one)

These are stored locally in:

- `~/Library/Application Support/StatFocus/sessions.json` — your session history
- `~/Library/Preferences/com.thiagogruber.statfocus.plist` — your preferences

## What we send over the network

**Nothing.** StatFocus makes no network requests. It does not call home, check for updates remotely, or contact any analytics service. The app works fully offline.

## Backups

If you enable the optional automatic backup feature, StatFocus writes a copy of your session history to the folder you choose on your own device. You control where that folder is and what happens to it. We do not have access to it.

## Permissions StatFocus may request

- **Files and folders** (when you choose a backup folder or import a backup) — strictly to read/write the file you selected
- **Notifications** (optional) — to alert you when focus sessions end
- **Login items** (optional) — to launch StatFocus when you log in

You can revoke any of these at any time in **System Settings → Privacy & Security**.

## Children

StatFocus does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children. The app is intended for general audiences.
